# Social Media Checklist for College Students

## ✅ Do’s
- Think before you post—make sure your content is appropriate and respectful.
- Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings and limiting personal information.
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
- Be respectful and positive in your interactions with others online.

## ❌ Don’ts
- Don’t share sensitive personal information (e.g., address, phone number, passwords).
- Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content that could harm your reputation.
- Don’t engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
- Don’t overshare your location or daily routines publicly.
- Don’t trust every link or message—avoid clicking on suspicious content.
