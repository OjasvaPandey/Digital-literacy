Social Media Checklist for College Students

1)-Do’s
- Think before you post , make sure your content is appropriate.
- Protect your privacy by adjusting account settings and also limit your personal information.
- Use strong passwords also enable two-factor authentication.
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading wrong information.
- Always keep good and positive interactions with others online.

2)-Don’ts
- Don’t share sensitive personal information (e.g., address, phone number, passwords).
- Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content that could harm your reputation.
- Don’t engage in cyberbullying or online arguments.
- Don’t overshare your location or daily routines publicly.
- Don’t trust every link or clicking on suspicious content.
